Our Story

Will's Home was founded in 2023 by William Mensah in response to the lack of educational resources in rural communities. Over the years, we have grown from a small community initiative into a recognized organization with a global reach.


Making a Difference

Since our inception, we have built 50 schools, protected 10,000 acres of rainforest, provided clean water to 20,000 families. Here are some of the stories from the people whose lives we’ve touched:

  • Empowering Women Through Education

    Amina, 32, from rural Kenya

    Amina grew up in a remote village in Kenya where access to education was limited, especially for girls. With the support of our educational outreach program, she received a scholarship to attend secondary school. Excelling in her studies, Amina went on to university, where she studied agricultural sciences. Today, she is a successful agronomist, helping local farmers improve their yields and sustain their livelihoods. Amina's journey from a small village to becoming a community leader and an advocate for education demonstrates the transformative power of providing educational opportunities

  • Overcoming Adversity Through Skill Development

    Carlos, 28, from Brazil

    Carlos faced numerous challenges growing up in a disadvantaged neighborhood in São Paulo. Unemployment and crime were rampant, and opportunities for a better life seemed scarce. Through our vocational training program, Carlos learned electrical engineering skills and obtained a certification. With determination and hard work, he secured a job with a reputable company. Today, Carlos not only supports his family but also mentors young people in his community, encouraging them to pursue education and skill development. His success story highlights the impact of vocational training in breaking the cycle of poverty and creating sustainable livelihoods

Our partners

We are grateful for the support of our partners and sponsors who help us achieve our mission. Here are some of the organizations we collaborate with:

  • Global Education Fund

    The Global Education Fund supports educational initiatives in underdeveloped regions, providing scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure development. Their generous contributions have enabled us to expand our educational outreach programs, ensuring more children have access to quality education.

  • Sustainable Agriculture Alliance

    The Sustainable Agriculture Alliance promotes environmentally friendly farming practices and supports local farmers with training and resources. Their partnership has been crucial in implementing our agricultural development projects, helping communities achieve food security and sustainable livelihoods.

  • Health for All Foundation

    The Health for All Foundation focuses on improving healthcare access in remote areas. Through their support, we have been able to organize medical camps, distribute essential medicines, and provide health education to underserved populations, significantly improving community health outcomes.
